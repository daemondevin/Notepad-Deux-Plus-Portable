Font(s) added here will be loaded on launch and accessible during runtime.

NOTE:
	The launcher will have to load and unload any fonts in this directory.
	The more fonts you have will mean a longer work load for the launcher.
	
Fonts Supported:
 • .fon
 • .fnt
 • .ttf
 • .ttc
 • .fot
 • .otf
 • .mmm
 • .pfb
 • .pfm
